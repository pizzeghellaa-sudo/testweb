Our Story

About MedEquip Supply

A dedicated supplier of medical equipment to hospitals, clinics, and healthcare procurement teams — built on transparency, traceability, and reliable service.

Our Mission

Supplying Healthcare with Confidence

We focus on quality-managed supply, clear documentation, and responsive support — giving procurement teams the information they need at every stage.

We supply medical equipment to healthcare providers, supporting procurement teams with traceable, quality-managed products. Our processes are structured to provide documentation, traceability, and compliance-ready records upon request.

From initial enquiry to post-delivery follow-up, we work alongside your team to match specifications, coordinate logistics, and ensure your facility receives reliable, well-documented equipment.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Four principles that guide how we operate and how we serve our clients.

Quality Management

Our supply processes follow quality-managed procedures, with documentation packages available for every product category.

Regulatory Support

We help procurement teams navigate documentation requirements, providing declarations of conformity and traceability records as needed.

Reliable Supply Chain

We maintain managed relationships with verified suppliers to ensure consistent product availability and predictable lead times.

Dedicated Service

Our team is responsive, accessible, and focused on long-term client relationships — not single transactions.

Our Commitment

What You Can Expect from Us

We make specific commitments to every client we work with.

  • Documentation on request — product specifications, declarations of conformity, and supply chain records available for every order.

  • Pre-sales technical support — our team assists with specification matching, product comparison, and procurement guidance before you commit.

  • Post-delivery follow-up — we check in after delivery to confirm receipt, address any issues, and support your acceptance process.
